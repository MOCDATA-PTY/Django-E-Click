How It Works

🔄 Restore Points

Create restore points before making major changes. Like Windows System Restore, you can roll back to any previous state.

📤 Export Data

Export your complete database to JSON format. Perfect for backups, migrations, or sharing data between systems.

🛡️ Safe Restoration

Every restore operation creates a safety backup first. Your current data is never lost during restoration.

📁 File Management

Upload existing backups, download restore points, and manage your backup collection with ease.
